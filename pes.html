PESTICIDES WASTE


A pesticide is a substance or mixture of substance intended for preventing, destroying, repelling or lessening the damage caused by the pest.

-> A pesticide can be a insect, plant pathogen, weed, bacteria, bird etc. That compete with the human for food, destroy property, spread disease.

-> A pesticide can be a chemical, biological agent, antimicrobial, disinfectant etc.

-> Many chemical pesticides are poisonous to human and animals.
